About

Come and work out or relax at Xcel Leisure. A state of the art facility in Dublin 9, Excel Leisure features a 12m Swimming pool,hydrotherapy pool with swim jets and a spa area with Jacuzzi, sauna and steam room Our Gym Floor has a fully equipped Free Weights Area, a Functional training area, Olympic lifting platforms with bumper plates. We also have a Pure strength room ,Spin studio, Boxing room and Class Studio. In addition to this, we have a full range of Technogym Cardio Vascular Exercise Equipment .

Parents of kids using our facilities can enjoy a work-out themselves or just relax and chat over a cup of coffee in our new café. Parents also have peace of mind knowing the kids are safe and well looked after by both their club organisers and the professional staff at Xcel Leisure Centre. Extensive CCTV coverage will also give additional peace of mind to parents.

Give us a call today or drop in for a guided tour. You won’t be disappointed with the range of equipment available, not to mention our selection of free classes included in your membership.
