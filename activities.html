High Performance Academy

Xcel Leisure in association with Spare Sports Partnership has established a High Performance Academy to support training and give financial aid to our local young athletes. Places are awarded on outstanding past performances and more importantly strong evidence of future achievements. The aim of the High Performance Academy is to give talented young people in our local community the opportunity of developing their sporting career by offering the highest standards in coaching, training, nutrition, strength and conditioning advice, while also going to school/college and living in the locality.
